Observations & Recommendations

Observation

The MoM (Minutes of Meeting) successfully filters out explicit and abusive words, generating clean summaries. In contrast, the raw transcription accurately captures and outputs the exact spoken text as it was delivered during the session.

Note on Highlighting

Some highlighted words are not inherently abusive (for example, words like 'غلطی' and 'نکمہ'); they are flagged because of how they are spoken or the context in which they appear during the session.

Recommendation

I recommend adding a sanitization layer between the raw MIH transcription and the displayed output, one that removes, masks, or replaces flagged language (for example, with ******). Both the original transcription and the sanitized version should be stored, so there is a complete audit trail and evidence is retained.
